Quick Start
################

What's included in the box?
****************************

When you purchase a brand new BeagleV Ahead, In the box you'll get:

1. BeagleV Ahead board
2. One (1) 2.4GHz/5GHz antennas
3. microUSB OTG cable
4. Quick-start card
